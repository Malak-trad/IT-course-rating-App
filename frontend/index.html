Academic Structure

University of Algiers follows the LMD system (License-Master-Doctorate)

License (Bachelor)

3-year undergraduate program (L1, L2, L3) in Computer Science

45 ECTS per year

Master

2-year graduate program (M1, M2) with specializations

60 ECTS total

Software Engineering, AI, Networks, Data Science
